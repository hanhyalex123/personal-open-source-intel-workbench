# UI Card Hierarchy Polish Design

Date: 2026-03-16

## Goals
- Enhance visual hierarchy for **Effective Config** and **Sync Log Detail** sections.
- Use stronger card treatment: borders + shadows + header emphasis.

## Approach
- Effective Config: white card with header chip, stronger border, soft shadow.
- Sync Log Detail: elevated card with section header, row separators, clearer typographic hierarchy.
- Keep consistent with current design system and color palette.

## Success Criteria
- The two sections read as first-class cards and visually separate from surrounding content.
- No layout regressions in Settings or Sync Log drawer.
